What to include

What will be deleted

Upon verification of your request, BoreMe will delete or de-identify account data and associated app data that can reasonably be linked to you, unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud prevention, billing, or compliance purposes.

What may be retained

Certain limited records may be retained where necessary for subscription reconciliation, fraud prevention, dispute handling, legal compliance, or enforcement of our terms.
